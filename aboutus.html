About Our Pharmacy

Where did we start?

We were founded in 1998 by two brothers who were pharmacists. They started this family-owned business in the old part of city of Prishtina, when there weren't many pharmacies around.

Our Mission.

Our Mission is to provide comprehensive pharmaceutical care to the residents within Prishtina. Meaning that we care not just about the availability of quality drug products and health care supplies, but more importantly, the availability of a qualified professional pharmacist to counsel patients, monitor drug therapy, and assist physicians and other health care professionals in maximizing the quality of these patients’ health care.

Our Costumers.

We reach and improve the health of communities across Prishtina through our local presence, digital channels and our dedicated colleagues – including physicians, pharmacists, and nurses.

What we offer

  • Free delivery
  • Competitive prescription pricing
  • Refills via phone or online
  • Expedient prescription fill time
  • Personal service
