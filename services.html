Covid - 19 Instructions
In case you are diagnosed with Covid-19 or you just have doubts; you can contact us at any time to take the necessary measures at the right time. In the meantime you can protect your self and the others around you by following the below - listed instructions:

Get vaccinated as soon as it’s your turn and follow local guidance on vaccination.

Keep physical distance of at least 1 metre from others, even if they don’t appear to be sick. Avoid crowds and close contact.

Wear a properly fitted mask when physical distancing is not possible and in poorly ventilated settings.

Clean your hands frequently with alcohol-based hand rub or soap and water.

Health education
Health education builds people's knowledge, skills, and positive attitudes about health. Health education teaches about physical, mental, emotional and social health. It motivates people to improve and maintain their health, prevent disease, and reduce risky behaviours. Health education is important to teach people awareness about living a healthy lifestyle. Health education encompasses social, mental, physical and social health. Health education teaches people of all ages about how diet and exercise contribute to a healthy lifestyle. It also encourages positive changes in behaviour and lowers the risk of addiction to drugs, alcohol and unsafe sexual practices.
