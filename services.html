Covid - 19 Instructions

In case you are diagnosed with Covid-19 or you just have doubts, you can protect your self and the others around you by following the below - listed instructions:

Get vaccinated as soon as it’s your turn and follow local guidance on vaccination.

Keep physical distance of at least 1 metre from others.

Wear a properly fitted mask when physical distancing is not possible and in poorly ventilated settings.

Clean your hands frequently with alcohol-based hand rub or soap and water.

Disposal and review of medicals

Besides selling the medicines, our pharmacy provides the service of disposal of medicals. Taking into consideration the high risk of using expired medications or the wrong ones, it is in our priority to ensure that you are taking the correct treatment and in time. So, please bring your expired medications in our pharmacy in order to dispose the bad ones, so in this way we can be sure that they don’t end up in the wrong hands.

Health education

Health education teaches about physical, mental, emotional and social health. It motivates people to improve and maintain their health, prevent disease, and reduce risky behaviours.It is important to teach people awareness about living a healthy lifestyle.It helps people of all ages about how diet and exercise contribute to a healthy lifestyle. It also encourages positive changes in behaviour and lowers the risk of addiction to drugs, etc.
